• Welcome to Sally O'Briens

    Sally O'Brien's is an Irish Pub opened by two Limerick lads with a passion for drinks and an even greater passion for the craic. A safe space for people to drink, laugh, dance and play. Everyone is welcome and shite talk is encouraged.
    We have ever-changing Guest Beers, Cocktails of the Month, a different Pure Daycent Daily Deal for every day of the week and Monthly Themed Parties that have so far included a Leap-Day-Pirate-Party, The Pub Game Olympics and a Mexican Star Wars Night. But even though you can expect an excellent pint of Guinness, delicious Irish Take-Away Style Food, Live Music and an epic Whiskey selection, Sally O’Brien’s is definitely Not Your Typical Irish Pub.
